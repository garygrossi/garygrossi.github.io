About me

I study computer engineering at Drexel University and practice software development in my free time. My technical interests are video game design, hardware prototyping, and application development. I’ve worked on several projects related to those interests and am always looking for new technical skills to pick up. I recently completed an internship at Scholly as a software developer. Outside of studying for classes I am currently an officer of the Drexel Game Developers Group and a lead organizer of DragonHacks.
